Research

My primary research and professional objective is to use data science and machine learning to contribute to the intersection of journalism and technology. I believe that there is currently a lack of software-driven approaches to collect and analyze large-scale corpora from digital communities. Thus, I am interested in applying natural language processing techniques to study discourses, behaviors and biases in digital communities at scale. More specifically, I aim to develop software tools for tasks such as critical discourse analysis, opinion mining, and subjectivity classification. Ultimately, I hope that my skill set and work can inform public policy, developmental economics and social change.
